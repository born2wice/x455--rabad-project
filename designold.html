When I Get Older

When I Get Older... is a book based on the life of Alessio Valenzuela Huelga. The pages are filled with colorful illustrations composed using various digital painting and compositing techniques on original photography by Alessio & Laurie Huelga. Story and illustrations created with love by Alessio’s oldest grandson, Raymond Abad, in Winter 2022 for the UC Berkeley Extension Children’s Book Illustration course; as part of the professional certificate in graphic design. My hope is that this book will be just one of many tools we can use to keep the legacy of La Bestia Negra alive!
